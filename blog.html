Festivities hit the town with a bang!

  • Charlotte
  • Dec 6, 2024
  • 2 Comments

As always, a great start to local Christmas celebrations was had by all at the family-friendly Deal Annual Christmas Lights Switch On for 2024!


Deal's town centre was jam-packed on Saturday 23rd November with locals and visitors gathering from late afternoon to enjoy music from local bands, good local produce, a visit from Father Christmas, ultimately ending with the light switch on outside St George's Hall.

Its always such a joy to see the community come together like this. These lights symbolize the warmth and spirit of Deal during the holidays - Deal Mayor
