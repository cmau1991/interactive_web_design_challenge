About

Learn More About Deal

Nestled on the English Channel, Deal is a charming seaside town in Kent known for its rich history, picturesque scenery, and relaxed atmosphere. Its blend of maritime heritage, quaint streets, and modern attractions makes it a delightful destination for visitors.

Top Attractions:


Deal Castle
  • Built by Henry VIII, this impressive Tudor artillery fort is a must-see. Its circular design and interactive exhibits offer a glimpse into the regions defense history.

    • Deal Pier
  • A landmark for over 60 years, the pier provides stunning sea views, a great spot for fishing, and a modern café at its end.

    • Walmer Castle and Gardens
  • Just a short walk from Deal, this historic fort transformed into a stately home with beautifully landscaped gardens.

    • Timeball Tower Museum
  • Learn about this historic maritime signaling device and its role in aiding ships' navigation.

    • Deal’s mix of history, natural beauty, and a welcoming community make it an ideal destination for a day trip or a longer stay. Whether you're a history buff, nature lover, or simply seeking a peaceful retreat, Deal offers something special for everyone.

    Natural Beauty and Outdoor Activities

  • Stroll along the pebble beach with iconic views of the white cliffs in the distance.
  • Explore Sandwich and Pegwell Bay Nature Reserve, a haven for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts.
  • Wander through the Kent Downs, an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty just a short drive away.

    • Culture and Shopping

  • Browse independent shops, galleries, and antique stores in Deals charming town centre.
  • Discover local cuisine at seaside pubs, cafés, and restaurants, offering everything from fish and chips to fine dining.
  • Experience the vibrant Saturday Market, a hub for local produce and crafts.

    • Events and Festivals

  • Deal hosts events throughout the year, including the Deal Music & Arts Festival and lively summer regattas.

    • Getting Here

  • Deal is easily accessible by train from London (around 1.5 hours) and well-connected to nearby coastal towns.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    How to get to Deal by train?

    It's easy to take a day trip to Deal from London and other parts of southern England. There are direct high-speed trains to Deal from the UK capital, and the journey takes less than an hour and a half.

    How to get to Deal by car?

    There's parking in Deal, too, so driving is an option if you prefer to take a day trip from London by car. The journey time from the capital is around two hours.
